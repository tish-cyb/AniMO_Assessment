TESDA Farming Courses & Certifications

TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority) offers various agricultural courses and training programs to equip aspiring and current farmers with essential skills for modern and sustainable farming.

  • Agricultural Crops Production NC I/II/III: Covers planting, caring, and harvesting of various crops.
  • Organic Agriculture Production NC II: Focuses on organic farming methods.
  • Animal Production (Poultry/Swine) NC II: Training in raising livestock.
  • Fishport/Seaport Operations NC I: Relevant for aquaculture and fisheries.

These courses lead to national certifications, enhancing employability and credibility in the agricultural sector.

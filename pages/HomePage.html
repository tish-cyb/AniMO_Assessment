What is RSBSA?

The Registry System for Basic Sectors in Agriculture (RSBSA) is a nationwide database of farmers and fisherfolk. It serves as a targeting mechanism for the Department of Agriculture (DA) and its attached agencies to provide appropriate interventions and assistance to the agriculture sector.

Who Can Register

  • Farmers (crop, livestock, poultry)
  • Fisherfolk (aquaculture, municipal, commercial)
  • Farmworkers / Fishworkers
  • Anyone involved in agriculture!

Relief for Our Farmers

RSBSA registration opens doors to various government programs, including financial assistance, insurance, training, and farm inputs. It's crucial for every farmer to be registered to avail these benefits.

Step-by-Step Procedure

It's Easy To Get Registered!

Visit your nearest Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO)

Bring a Valid Government ID

PhilSys, Barangay Certificate, Passport

Fill out the RSBSA registration form

Wait for confirmation and receive your RSBSA ID number

Once You Have Your RSBSA ID:

  • Keep it safe and accessible.
  • Use it when applying for DA programs.
  • Ensure your information is up-to-date.

What is RSBSA?

The Registry System for Basic Sectors in Agriculture (RSBSA) is an official list of farmers, fishers, and farm laborers who are eligible for government programs and support.

Who Can Register

Filipino citizens

18 years old and above

Agri-youth (18+ involved in farming)

Fisherfolks
